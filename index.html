Design - Trojan Rebrand
This assignment required rebranding an existing brand in need of revitalization, with a goal to effectively reposition it and compellingly present our concept. Working collaboratively with a classmate, I took the lead in directing the creative vision, designing the logo, packaging, typography, and color palette. Our rebrand strategically positioned Trojan as a luxury, contemporary product tailored specifically toward a younger demographic. The final deliverables included a refined visual identity, targeted slogans, social media assets, and a promotional video spot. We received excellent feedback on this project, notably for successfully communicating a modern, sleek aesthetic, resulting in an outstanding grade.
Video/Audio - Documentary
The goal of this assignment was to create a concise, impactful documentary that explores an important issue. Our group chose to investigate the mental health epidemic affecting students at the University of Waterloo, with a specific focus on the effectiveness of campus mental health services. Serving as co-director, I led the filmmaking process, conducted all interviews, guided the conversations, shaped the narrative structure, filmed extensively, and played a significant role in editing the documentary. The final product received strong praise from our professor for its compelling storytelling and powerful message, earning an excellent grade.
