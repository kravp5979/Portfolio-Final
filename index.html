This poster series is me, as a designer, trying to find my creative identity. Each piece is a reflection of something that I’ve always considered design. The first, "I Will," is one of ambition and resolve, manifested in big typeface and a large self-portrait. Second part: "Who is Peter?" - layers collage to tell my creative story and inspiration behind my style. And last but not least "Good Style is Paradoxical", that abstract, dynamic composition shows the dualistic aspect of my design ethos. It was a project that merged my own personal narrative with artistic play, which proved I could speak visually with my bold ideas and contrasting imagery.
Design - Trojan Rebrand
This was a branding and visual identity rewrite for the Trojan condom brand. I started by looking at the brand as it is, and determining where it is failing and comparing it to competitors to see where it could improve. From here, I designed a new logo, packaging and brand identity with fresh typography and bold color palette. The rebrand was accompanied by a marketing campaign including slogans, social media content, and a short video spot. Through this whole project, I learned a lot about branding, design, and Adobe software like Illustrator, InDesign, and Express.
Video/Audio - Documentary
This documentary examines the mental health epidemic in university students with a focus on our school and health care services. Alongside my team, we determined what drove mental health crises and reviewed university resources. Interviews and studies will help inform, provoke discussion and call for better mental health services on campus. This project enhanced my expertise in a group filmmaking and storytelling.
